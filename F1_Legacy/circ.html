Formula 1 Circuits

Explore the legendary tracks that challenge the world's best drivers.

2025 Formula 1 Grand Prix Circuits

FORMULA 1 LOUIS VUITTON AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Melbourne, Australia
Circuit: Albert Park Circuit

A semi-permanent street circuit known for its fast, flowing sections and challenging braking zones. It combines public roads with purpose-built track sections, offering a unique blend of street circuit feel and traditional racing.

FORMULA 1 HEINEKEN CHINESE GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Shanghai, China
Circuit: Shanghai International Circuit

Famous for its unique 'snail' shaped Turn 1-2 complex and long back straight. This modern Hermann Tilke-designed track offers a variety of corner speeds and good overtaking opportunities.

FORMULA 1 LENOVO JAPANESE GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Suzuka, Japan
Circuit: Suzuka International Racing Course

Celebrated for its unique figure-of-eight layout, challenging high-speed S-curves, and iconic Degner curves. It's a favorite among drivers for its flow and technical demands, often hosting crucial championship deciders.

FORMULA 1 GULF AIR BAHRAIN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Sakhir, Bahrain
Circuit: Bahrain International Circuit

A modern desert circuit featuring a mix of high-speed sections and challenging low-speed corners. It's known for its abrasive surface and often hosts the season-opening night race under floodlights.

FORMULA 1 STC SAUDI ARABIAN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Jeddah, Saudi Arabia
Circuit: Jeddah Corniche Circuit

The fastest street circuit on the F1 calendar, characterized by its numerous high-speed corners and narrow sections. It's a thrilling challenge that demands extreme precision and courage.

FORMULA 1 CRYPTO.COM MIAMI GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Miami Gardens, USA
Circuit: Miami International Autodrome

A purpose-built street circuit set around Hard Rock Stadium. It features a unique combination of high-speed straights, medium-speed corners, and a slow, technical chicane. Known for its vibrant atmosphere.

FORMULA 1 AWS GRAN PREMIO DEL MADE IN ITALY E DELL'EMILIA-ROMAGNA 2025

Location: Imola, Italy
Circuit: Autodromo Internazionale Enzo e Dino Ferrari

A classic, old-school circuit with a rich history, known for its demanding chicanes, high-speed sections, and a flowing layout that punishes mistakes. It's a driver's favorite due to its challenging nature.

FORMULA 1 TAG HEUER GRAND PRIX DE MONACO 2025

Location: Monte Carlo, Monaco
Circuit: Circuit de Monaco

The iconic street circuit of Monaco is synonymous with glamour and precision. Its tight corners, narrow passages, and elevation changes make it one of the most demanding and prestigious races on the calendar, where qualifying is king.

FORMULA 1 ARAMCO GRAN PREMIO DE ESPAÑA 2025

Location: Montmeló, Spain
Circuit: Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya

A well-rounded circuit used extensively for testing, featuring a mix of high-speed and technical sections. It's a good all-rounder that tests a car's overall performance and a driver's consistency.

FORMULA 1 PIRELLI GRAND PRIX DU CANADA 2025

Location: Montreal, Canada
Circuit: Circuit Gilles Villeneuve

A semi-permanent street circuit known for its long straights, tight chicanes, and the famous 'Wall of Champions'. It often produces exciting races with good overtaking opportunities.

FORMULA 1 MSC CRUISES AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Spielberg, Austria
Circuit: Red Bull Ring

A compact and picturesque circuit set in the Styrian mountains. It features short straights, hard braking zones, and a flowing middle sector, leading to close racing and plenty of action.

FORMULA 1 QATAR AIRWAYS BRITISH GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Silverstone, United Kingdom
Circuit: Silverstone Circuit

A historic circuit known for its high-speed corners like Copse, Maggotts, and Becketts, Silverstone is a true test of a car's aerodynamic prowess and a driver's bravery. It's often referred to as the "home of British motorsport."

FORMULA 1 MOËT & CHANDON BELGIAN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Stavelot, Belgium
Circuit: Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps

A favorite among drivers, Spa is known for its dramatic elevation changes, unpredictable weather, and legendary corners like Eau Rouge and Blanchimont. It's the longest track on the F1 calendar and offers thrilling racing.

FORMULA 1 LENOVO HUNGARIAN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Mogyoród, Hungary
Circuit: Hungaroring

Often described as "Monaco without the buildings," the Hungaroring is a tight and twisty circuit with few overtaking opportunities, making qualifying and strategy critical. It's a physically demanding race for drivers.

FORMULA 1 HEINEKEN DUTCH GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Zandvoort, Netherlands
Circuit: Circuit Zandvoort

A historic circuit famous for its fast, banked corners and challenging undulations. Located near the coast, it's a tight and exhilarating track that puts car balance and driver skill to the test.

FORMULA 1 PIRELLI GRAN PREMIO D’ITALIA 2025

Location: Monza, Italy
Circuit: Autodromo Nazionale Monza

Affectionately called the "Temple of Speed," Monza is characterized by its long straights and fast chicanes. It's the spiritual home of Ferrari and a place where engine power and low-downforce setups are crucial for success.

FORMULA 1 QATAR AIRWAYS AZERBAIJAN GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Baku, Azerbaijan
Circuit: Baku City Circuit

A thrilling street circuit that blends extremely long straights with incredibly tight, medieval old city sections. It's known for producing unpredictable races and dramatic overtakes.

FORMULA 1 SINGAPORE AIRLINES SINGAPORE GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Singapore
Circuit: Marina Bay Street Circuit

The original night race in F1, Marina Bay is a dazzling street circuit set against the stunning Singapore skyline. Its bumpy surface, high humidity, and numerous turns make it a physically demanding and visually spectacular event.

FORMULA 1 MSC CRUISES UNITED STATES GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Austin, USA
Circuit: Circuit of the Americas (COTA)

A modern circuit inspired by classic European tracks, featuring a challenging uphill run to Turn 1 and a mix of high-speed esses and tight hairpins. It's designed to promote overtaking.

FORMULA 1 GRAN PREMIO DE LA CIUDAD DE MÉXICO 2025

Location: Mexico City, Mexico
Circuit: Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez

Known for its high altitude, which heavily impacts car performance, and the stadium section with its passionate crowd. The circuit combines fast straights with slow, technical corners.

FORMULA 1 MSC CRUISES GRANDE PRÊMIO DE SÃO PAULO 2025

Location: São Paulo, Brazil
Circuit: Autódromo José Carlos Pace (Interlagos)

A short, undulating circuit with a counter-clockwise layout and challenging elevation changes. It's famous for its dramatic races, often influenced by unpredictable weather.

FORMULA 1 HEINEKEN LAS VEGAS GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Las Vegas, USA
Circuit: Las Vegas Street Circuit

A spectacular night street race on the iconic Las Vegas Strip, featuring long straights for high top speeds and sharp corners for overtakes, all set against a vibrant city backdrop.

FORMULA 1 QATAR AIRWAYS QATAR GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Lusail, Qatar
Circuit: Lusail International Circuit

A relatively flat circuit known for its medium to high-speed corners, reminiscent of tracks like Mugello. It often presents challenges with sand on track and high temperatures.

FORMULA 1 ETIHAD AIRWAYS ABU DHABI GRAND PRIX 2025

Location: Abu Dhabi, UAE
Circuit: Yas Marina Circuit

A modern circuit known for its futuristic facilities, twilight race, and recent layout changes designed to improve racing. It features a mix of tight corners, medium-speed sections, and two long straights.
